Welcome to SWAT!

Follow the tabs above to administer your Samba server:

  • The conf page offers editing capabilities for global smb.conf parameters, as well as an overall view of the smb.conf file, possible parameters, and their default values.
  • The services page offers editing capabilities for file and printer share parameters. File and printer shares may also be added in this section.
  • The status page offers controls for starting or restarting the Samba binaries. Information is also available on active connections, shares, and open files.
  • The passwd page offers controls for changing user passwords on both the server and the client, as well as options for adding/deleting/enabling/disabling users.

SWAT's Samba Configure Wizard can help with the intitial setup of your Samba server.

And help is available from any page, by following the question mark in the upper right-hand corner.
